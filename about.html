Discover the team

Hi, let us introduce ourselves! We are the team behind SKYS.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, a group of students decided to launch a travel company. We all experience loneliness, anxiousness and an overall lower state of happiness because the world has been shut down for quite a while. In order to fix this, we came up with SKYS. SKYS is a website which helps you book your new adventure, without having to worry about COVID restrictions or safety!

All 5 of us are extremely passionate about exploring new places, meeting new people and experiencing new adventures. Because we ourselves seek adventure so much, but we couldn’t really find a website which helps us find these adventures, we decided to create one ourselves. SKYS is for everyone who seeks new experiences and adventure! Remember: Life begins at the end of your comfort zone!
